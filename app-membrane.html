Polyurelyuthane Flooringthane flooring is a tough, flexible coating made from polyurethane resin, often used in industrial and commercial spaces. It resists heavy wear, impact, and chemicals, making it ideal for high-traffic areas. The floor provides a seamless, customizable finish, with options for a glossy, matte, or satin look.

The application process involves preparing the concrete base, pouring or spreading the polyurethane mixture, and allowing it to cure. Multiple layers can be applied for added durability. Once cured, the floor is sealed for protection and easy maintenance, commonly used in factories, hospitals, and kitchens.
